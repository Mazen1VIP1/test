My Favourite Animal is the cat

The cat is a small, cute, and active pet that loves to play, sleep, and has sharp senses.

Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats sleep about 13 to 16 hours a day.
  • They can jump up to six times their body length.
  • They use their tails to keep balance.
